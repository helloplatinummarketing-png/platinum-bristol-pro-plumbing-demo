@AGENTS.md

You are building premium websites for UK trades businesses — plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers and builders across the UK.

Every website must:
- Use Framer Motion for all animations
- Be mobile-first and optimised for phone viewing
- Load fast with no unnecessary libraries
- Have a clear hero section with one CTA above the fold
- Include social proof such as reviews or testimonials
- Have a clear pricing or services section
- End with a strong call to action
- Use dark professional colour schemes unless client specifies otherwise
- Be deployable to Vercel with one command

Target audience is busy UK tradespeople and their customers. Keep copy clear, direct and benefit-focused. Never use jargon.

Stack: Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, shadcn/ui Radix Nova components, TypeScript.

When building a new client site always ask for:
1. Business name and owner name
2. Trade and location
3. Key services offered
4. Average job value
5. Number of Google reviews currently
6. Current biggest problem — missed calls, no-shows, cold quotes, or invoice chasing
7. Colour preferences
8. Any existing logo or branding
